Who is an Entreprenur

A entrepreneur simply refers to someone who runs or operate a business. entrepreneur are persons who possess to see and evaluate business opportunites, put together the necessary resources to take advantages of them and initiate appropriate actions to ensure success

Etrepreneurial Role

Roles here are the responsibilities which an enrepreneur is expected to perform in the running of the business or the organisation set of behavior associated with his or her position. These roles are categorised into three namely; interpersonal relation, information processing and decision making
